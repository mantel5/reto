We're looking for a Web Application Developer to join our **The Last Burger** family. If you're passionate about technology and want to contribute to the development of our digital platforms, this opportunity is for you!

Vacancy: Web Application Developer

Minimum Education:

  • University degree in Systems Engineering, Programming, or related disciplines.
  • Additional training (courses, certifications, etc.) in web technologies will be an asset.

Languages:

  • Intermediate level of English (B1 or higher), in both written and oral skills.
  • Other languages will be considered favorably.

Specific Knowledge:

  • Proficiency in programming languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and current frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js, among others).
  • Experience in backend development (Node.js, Express, Django, etc.).
  • Knowledge in SQL and NoSQL database systems (MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL).
  • Knowledge of version control tools (Git, GitHub/GitLab).
  • Knowledge of agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).

Experience:

  • At least 2 years of verifiable experience building web applications.
  • Experience working in collaborative environments and groups of diverse disciplines.

Job Skills and Abilities:

  • Problem solving and analytical reasoning skills.
  • Effective communication and group collaboration.
  • Desire to acquire knowledge and adjust to technological innovations.
  • Proactive and responsible behavior.
  • Abilities to handle tasks independently.
  • Position Category:
  • Junior or Senior Web Application Developer (depending on experience).

Job Duties:

  • Creating and supporting web applications, covering both frontend and backend.
  • Work with the design and UX/UI team to develop effective and attractive user interfaces.
  • Execute tests and fix code bugs.
  • Manage and improve application efficiency.
  • Manage databases and ensure the integrity and protection of information.
  • Develop creative solutions in software initiatives.

Job Requirements:

Salary:

  • According to experience and skills (between €24,000 and €40,000 per year).
  • Opportunity for value increase after performance evaluation.

    • Working hours:

  • Full time.
  • Availability of flexible hours and the possibility of remote work (advanced remote work skills will be a benefit).

Election Process:

  • Application Submission: Send your updated CV, portfolio (if you have one) and motivation letter to our Human Resources email.
  • Evaluation of applications: We will analyze your profile according to the requirements and previous experience.
  • Technical interview: A conversation in which we will discuss your technical skills and solutions to practical problems.
  • Individual interview: We want to get to know you better, talk about your professional behavior and how you would integrate into the team.
  • Final proposal: If you are selected, we will make you an official job offer including all the details.

If you meet the requirements and are interested in joining our team, we look forward to hearing from you!
