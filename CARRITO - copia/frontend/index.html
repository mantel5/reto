Frequently Asked Questions

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver through our own service and also work with major delivery platforms.

Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes, we have several vegetarian and vegan options on our menu, including our famous Veggie Burger.

Can I make reservations?

We do not accept reservations as we work on a first-come, first-served basis, except for groups larger than 8 people.

Do you have parking?

Our Downtown branch has its own parking lot. Public parking is available nearby at other branches.

Can I customize my burger?

Absolutely! You can request any modification to our burgers at no additional cost.
